# AI Video Brain: Editing Blueprint

**Platform:** all
**Conversion Goal:** leads

---

## 1. Brand Compliance Matrix

**Brand Summary:** TechFlow embodies a professional and approachable SaaS technology brand targeting tech-savvy entrepreneurs and startup founders. The brand emphasizes innovation, trustworthiness, and clarity, using a clean and efficient visual style with a blue-centric color palette and modern typography. Communications maintain simplicity and avoid jargon, fostering an empowering and equal dialogue with its audience.

**Tone of Voice:** Professional yet approachable, innovative, trustworthy

## 2. Emotional Arc & Pacing

**Overall Tone:** Trust/Authority

**Hook Strategy:** Open with a relatable pain point about wasting time on repetitive tasks to immediately engage viewers who face this frustration.

**Editing Rhythm:** Start with a moderate pace to build empathy and curiosity, accelerate briefly during the excitement peak, then moderate again to reinforce trust, and finish with a brisk pace to drive urgency and conversion.

## 3. Visual & Compositional Recommendations

### B-Roll Shot List:

1. **0:00-0:10** - Close-up shots of a frustrated entrepreneur at their cluttered desk, rubbing forehead or looking overwhelmed by repetitive tasks on computer screens.
   - Type: Specific
   - Composition: Rule of Thirds to place subject off-center, emphasizing space filled with cluttered work environment

2. **0:10-0:20** - Medium shot of the entrepreneur’s hand interacting with a sleek tablet showing the TechFlow AI assistant interface; screen shows dynamic, intuitive UI elements.
   - Type: Specific
   - Composition: Leading Lines directing viewer’s eye from hand to screen interface

3. **0:20-0:30** - Fast-paced montage of the entrepreneur completing automation tasks in 3 clicks; UI animations highlight simplicity and speed.
   - Type: Specific
   - Composition: Fill the Frame with UI and hand movements to emphasize ease and focus

4. **0:30-0:40** - Confident entrepreneur smiling, relaxed, working in a bright modern office; overlay of customer testimonials or stats subtly integrated.
   - Type: Broad
   - Composition: Central Composition to convey trust and authority

5. **0:40-0:50** - Close-up of a countdown timer or limited offer graphic on screen, entrepreneur clicking ‘Try Now’ button; urgency emphasized by quick cuts.
   - Type: Specific
   - Composition: Symmetry for button and timer placement to draw focus

## 4. Conversion & Engagement Optimization

**Primary Psychological Trigger:** Curiosity

### CTA Placements:

- **Pre-roll** (0:04): Discover how to automate your work in 3 clicks!
- **Mid-roll** (0:25): Ready to save 15 hours/week? Try TechFlow Free!
- **Post-roll** (0:48): Start your 14-day free trial now – No credit card required!
