# DIGITAL HUMAN PRESENCE SOP: THE INFINITE SALES PROTOCOL

**Author:** Unwind Code
**System:** Digital Human Presence Engine (Phase 9)
**Purpose:** To define the operational procedures for an autonomous digital persona to generate content, engage audiences, and execute sales prospecting.

---

## 1. Presence Protocol: Content Distribution

The process begins once the **Autonomous Assembly Engine (Phase 8)** outputs the final video file.

### Protocol 1.1: Multi-Platform Posting
The **Digital Human Presence Engine (DHPE)** executes the following:
1.  **Metadata Generation:** Generates platform-specific titles, descriptions, and tags based on the **Unwind Blueprint**.
2.  **Autonomous Upload:** Uses the Distribution MCPs (P9-T002) to post the video to YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram simultaneously.
3.  **Initial Engagement:** Automatically likes and pins the first comment (the CTA) to drive initial engagement.

## 2. Prospecting Protocol: Lead Generation & Scoring

The system actively monitors the performance of the live content to identify and qualify leads.

### Protocol 2.1: Engagement Monitoring
1.  **Data Fetching:** The Video Analytics MCP (P5-T005) fetches real-time engagement data (comments, shares, DMs).
2.  **Lead Identification:** The **Prospecting & Sales Cell (P9-T003)** identifies users who interact with the content, specifically those who respond to the CTA.

### Protocol 2.2: Lead Scoring & Qualification
The Sales Cell scores each lead based on:
*   **Engagement Depth:** Did they just like, or did they comment/DM?
*   **Intent Analysis:** Does their comment/DM indicate a high intent to purchase or learn more?
*   **Profile Audit:** Does the user's profile match the target audience defined in the Brand Analyst's guidelines?

## 3. Sales Protocol: Automated Outreach

High-scoring leads are moved into the automated outreach funnel.

### Protocol 3.1: Initial Outreach
1.  **Personalized DM/Email:** The Sales Cell generates a personalized outreach message based on the user's specific interaction and the **Invicta** mindset.
2.  **CRM Logging:** The lead and the outreach attempt are logged in the CRM MCP (P9-T004).

### Protocol 3.2: Escalation
If a lead responds positively, the system escalates the interaction to a human closer or a more advanced sales agent, providing a full context brief of the interaction history.

## 4. Feedback Protocol: Closing the Infinite Loop

The **Infinite Mirror** audits the entire sales cycle.

1.  **Conversion Audit:** The Mirror calculates the **Conversion Metric** (leads generated vs. sales closed).
2.  **Strategic Refinement:** If the conversion rate is low, the Mirror generates a **Growth Vector** to refine the **Script Formula** for the next video, ensuring the system is always optimizing for the ultimate goal: **Infinite Sales Presence.**
