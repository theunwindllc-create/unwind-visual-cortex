## Challenges Encountered

### Challenge 1: Blueprint to AE Data Mapping
**Description:** The Unwind blueprint is highly detailed and conceptual, while After Effects requires a very specific, structured data format. Mapping the two required careful design to ensure all key creative and strategic elements were preserved in a usable format.
**Resolution:** ✅ Designed a clean, hierarchical JSON structure that separates brand identity, text content, timing, and B-roll. This makes it easy for AE expressions to access specific data points without complex logic.
**Impact:** The resulting JSON is both comprehensive and easy to work with in After Effects, enabling a smooth data-driven workflow.

---

## Key Learnings & Insights

1. **Data Structure is Everything:** The success of a data-driven workflow hinges on a well-designed JSON structure. A clear, predictable format makes writing AE expressions much simpler.
2. **Expressions are Powerful but Verbose:** After Effects expressions can do almost anything, but they can become complex. Providing a library of pre-written expressions is crucial for user adoption.
3. **Hex to RGB Normalization is a Key Step:** After Effects expressions require colors in a normalized RGB array (e.g., `[1, 0.5, 0]`), not hex codes. This conversion must be handled by the exporter.
