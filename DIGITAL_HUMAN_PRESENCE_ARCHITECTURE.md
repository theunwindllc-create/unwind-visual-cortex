## 2. Integration of New Components

### A. The Digital Human Presence Engine (DHPE)
The DHPE is the orchestrator for the Avatar's real-world activities.

*   **Function:** Manages social media API keys, schedules posts, and handles the initial layer of audience interaction (likes, simple replies).
*   **Tools:** Custom MCPs for social media platforms (e.g., YouTube API, Instagram Graph API).

### B. The Autonomous Prospecting & Sales Cell
This cell is the "business brain" of the digital human.

*   **Function:** Analyzes engagement data (from P5-T005) to score leads, initiates automated outreach (DMs, emails), and qualifies prospects based on a defined sales funnel.
*   **Integration:** Requires a new **CRM MCP** (e.g., HubSpot or Salesforce) to log and track leads.

### C. The Infinite Mirror's Final Audit
The Mirror's Discipline Score (P7-T001) is expanded to include a **Conversion Metric**.

*   **Conversion Metric (30%):** Measures the lead-to-sale conversion rate generated by the Digital Human's content. A low score triggers a Growth Vector to refine the Script Formula's CTA strategy.
